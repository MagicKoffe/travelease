Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button in the header or visit the Sign Up page, fill out the required fields, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation email shortly after.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) as well as PayPal. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Can I cancel or change my booking?

You can modify or cancel bookings up to 24 hours before your scheduled trip without penalty. Visit your profile page to manage your bookings.

How do I contact customer support?

For any questions, please email support@travelease.com or use the chat icon in the bottom-right corner of the site.
